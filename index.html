Workshop Philosophy

Differentiation, Not Separation

All pathways cover the same core skills but approach them differently. This ensures everyone leaves with a common baseline whilst respecting your time and expertise.

Discipline-Agnostic Design

Tasks are deliberately general. You are encouraged to adapt examples and scenarios to your specific field (Law, Business, Criminology, Youth Work, Policing, etc.).

Critical Engagement

This is not a "pro-AI" workshop. You will actively test these tools for accuracy, bias, and ethical robustness. The goal is informed, critical adoption‚Äînot uncritical enthusiasm.

Safe to Experiment

There are no wrong questions. This is a space to try things, break things, and learn from both successes and failures. Facilitators are here to help at any point.
