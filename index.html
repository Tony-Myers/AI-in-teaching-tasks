Applied AI in Higher Education

A practical, multi-pathway workshop for academics across disciplines. Hands-on tasks, critical reflection, and ready-to-use artefacts for teaching.

Outcome

Image generation & bias

Create and critically evaluate images for teaching materials, identifying stereotypes and contextual mismatches.

Outcome

Source-grounded AI

Use NotebookLM to summarise and create audio from your own documents, with verifiable citations.

Outcome

Custom agents

Work with specialist Copilot agents (Rigour, Socratic, Feedback) and critique their behaviour.

Outcome

Student support

Draft guidance and assessment-aligned activities that promote responsible, effective student use.
